In 2022, I worked with the perception team to create the vision pipeline, generated synthetic data to train unseen object instance segmentation models using state of the art methods out of Fox Lab and NVIDIA, and deployed the system on our robotic arm for an evaluation led by Amazon, exceeding existing benchmarks.
